About Us

Leading the Way in Sustainable Trade

ZS GLOBAL FZE is a premier used goods exporting company based in Sharjah, UAE. We specialize in sourcing high-quality second-hand items and delivering them to markets across Africa, Asia, and beyond.

Our mission is to reduce textile waste while providing affordable clothing and goods to communities worldwide. Every item we export is carefully inspected, graded, and packed to ensure maximum value for our clients.
